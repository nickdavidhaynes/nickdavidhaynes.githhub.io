Publications

Current projects

  • Reservoir computing with a single time-delay autonomous Boolean node [PDF]

    N.D. Haynes, M.C. Soriano, D.P. Rosin, I. Fischer, D.J. Gauthier, Physical Review E 91 020801 (2015).

    One-sentence abstract: We built a "network" out of a single digital logic element with feedback and showed that it can be trained to recognize simple input patterns.

  • Transient scaling and resurgence of chimera states in coupled Boolean phase oscillators [PDF]

    D.P. Rosin, D. Rontani, N.D. Haynes, E. Schöll, D.J. Gauthier, Physical Review E 90 030902 (2014).

    One-sentence abstract: We experimentally observed order and disorder coexisting (called chimera states) in complex networks of high-speed digital electronics.
